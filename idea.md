# CONTEXT
- F&B business admin workflow automation using AI. 

# PROBLEM
- Business owner spending too much time on admin and not able to scale the business. 

# FEATURES
## 1. HR
- Payslip: auto issue of payslip 
- Payroll: setup salary scale and others to calculate payslip
- Timesheet: Digital timesheet interface for part-timers to clock in their hours
- Leave System: Leave and off applications

## 2. Expense Tracker
- Upload: Scan receipts uploaded, auto detect supplier and categorise items (ingredients, cleaning supplies), capture amount, GST, and invoice due date
- Inventorise: detected items can be prompted whether to inventorise
- Manual entry: certain expenese has no receipts, require manual entry shortcut from homescreen, quick select supplier and enter items or costs
- Camera: take photo and upload
- Reconciliation: able to upload all payments made records and tally with expense invoices, ensuring payment made on time. Inform payment overdue and almost due. Needed a summary. 

## 3. Accounting
- Upload POS data: enable upload of ePOS exported data
- Generate cashflow, balance sheet and p&l

## 4. Inventory
- Monthly stock take: How much left, so that balance sheet can account for perishable (with shelf life), and when to use them up and how much to restock. 
- Inventory prediction: how much to buy, how much waste

## 5. Misc
- Vouchers issued by landlord to consumers, consumer used to buy, F&B owner got to claim, voucher have unique number, account for it, tabulate monthly total for claim.
